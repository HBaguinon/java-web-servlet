Fraction of the Moon Illuminated on One Day

This data service calculates the fraction of the Moon's visible disk
illuminated by the Sun for every day in a selected year between 1700 and
2100. The computed fraction is a quantitative way of describing the Moon's
phase. Simply fill in the form below and click on the "Submit" button.
